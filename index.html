How Creative Coloring Works

1. Pick a Picture

Start by browsing our gallery of fun and cute images. Let your child choose their favorite picture, whether it's an animal, a vehicle, or a simple shape, to begin their coloring adventure.

2. Choose Your Colors

The core of the game is simple: tap a color from the palette, then tap the area of the picture you want to fill. It's an easy and intuitive "paint bucket" style perfect for little fingers.

3. Create a Masterpiece

There's no pressure or time limit. Encourage your child to experiment with different colors and bring their picture to life. The only goal is to have fun and be creative!

4. Save and Share

Once the artwork is complete, you can easily save it to your device. Share your child's colorful creations with family and friends or print it out to display on the fridge!
